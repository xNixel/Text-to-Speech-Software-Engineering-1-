Features

It focuses on creating a bidirectional text-to-speech recognition webpage meaning the said function can be used both ways, text-to-speech can be used by typing certain English words by the user and pressing a button to initiate the conversion of text into audio that can be played by the user while with speech-to-text the user will be asked to say a word and with the help of a voice recognition software/tool the recorded word will then be converted into text that can be read. 

Text to Speech

This will transform any text into audio that it recognizes the English language as it uses text as an input and outputs it to audio

Speech to Text

This will transform any audio to text that it recognizes your voice as it uses your speech as an input and audio speech as an output

Why use ITAC?

This website allows you to input your text and convert it to speech wherein, it helps by giving the proper pronunciation and spelling of words. People can input unfamiliar words in the text and output them in audio making the proper pronunciation of the word known also by voicing out unfamiliar words, it can output the proper spelling of words through text. This will increase communication between people with disabilities and non-disabled people since those who want to speak with them can utilize it as well. In terms of health, those who are muted can just input what they want to say, and it will be converted into audio. Probably speaking will compel it to translate their words into text that those who are muted may read.
